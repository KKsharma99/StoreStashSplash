Discover what all the buzz is about!


Many students here at Georgia Tech, especially out-of-state and international students, need a place to store their items over the summer. These might include cookware, blankets, books, or furniture. One common solution is to store their items in a storage unit, which costs upwards of $60 per month for a 5 feet by 5 feet unit. For people who need less space, this storage unit can be split among friends who trust each other enough to share the keys to the unit. Since storage units are often far away and may be difficult to access for students without cars, companies like Dorm Room Movers offer pickups and charge by item size at a marked up price. Alternatively, students may choose to sell them off at a loss in a Facebook group, and try to buy a similar item during the start of the next semester. StoreStash offers a gig economy where people with extra storage space could take in items for half the price of a storage unit and make money passively.
